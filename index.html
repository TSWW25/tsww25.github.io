Overview

The workshop titled "Towards a Safer Web for Women: First International Workshop on Protecting Women Online" is part of The Web Conference 2025 hosted in Sydney, Australia. The workshop date will be announced soon.

We invite researchers to submit their work to this workshop. Submissions can address a wide range of topics, including technological solutions, interdisciplinary strategies, and global perspectives on combating online violence against women. For detailed submission guidelines and topics of interest, please visit our Call for Papers page.
