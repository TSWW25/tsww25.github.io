Towards a Safer Web for Women: First International Workshop on Protecting Women Online

Web Conference 2025

Overview

This workshop focuses on addressing the challenges and solutions for protecting women in online spaces. It will bring together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to discuss the latest advancements and best practices.
